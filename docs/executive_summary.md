# Executive Summary

## Business Context

A SaaS company experienced slower-than-expected revenue growth despite maintaining healthy acquisition volumes.

Leadership requested an investigation into the customer acquisition funnel to identify:

- Conversion bottlenecks
- Underperforming customer segments
- Revenue leakage opportunities
- High-impact optimization initiatives

The analysis evaluated customer progression from acquisition through paid conversion.

---

## Objectives

1. Measure funnel conversion performance.
2. Identify drop-off points.
3. Compare acquisition channels.
4. Compare device performance.
5. Quantify lost revenue opportunities.
6. Recommend actions with measurable business impact.

---

## Key Findings

### Finding 1: Funnel Leakage Concentrated in Trial-to-Paid Stage

The largest conversion drop occurred between trial and paid subscription.

Business Impact:

- Marketing spend generates trial users successfully.
- Revenue realization is limited by poor monetization performance.

---

### Finding 2: Device Performance Gap

Desktop users converted significantly better than mobile users.

Business Impact:

- Mobile onboarding experience is likely creating friction.
- Revenue opportunity exists by reducing mobile drop-off.

---

### Finding 3: Acquisition Channel Quality Varies

Not all acquisition channels produce equal customer quality.

Some channels generate volume but lower paid conversion.

Business Impact:

- Marketing budget allocation should consider revenue efficiency, not just traffic.

---

### Finding 4: Revenue Recovery Opportunity Exists

Improving underperforming segments to benchmark conversion levels could generate meaningful incremental monthly recurring revenue.

Business Impact:

- Funnel optimization is more cost-effective than acquiring additional traffic.

---

## Recommendations

### Priority 1

Improve mobile onboarding experience.

Expected Outcome:

- Increased activation rate
- Increased trial conversion
- Higher MRR

---

### Priority 2

Optimize trial-to-paid conversion.

Potential Actions:

- Improve onboarding education
- Introduce in-app prompts
- Refine pricing communication

---

### Priority 3

Reallocate acquisition spend toward higher-converting channels.

Expected Outcome:

- Improved customer acquisition efficiency
- Higher revenue per acquired user

---

## Executive Conclusion

The primary business opportunity is not increasing acquisition volume.

The primary opportunity is improving conversion efficiency among existing users.

Even modest improvements in activation and paid conversion rates can generate significant recurring revenue growth while reducing acquisition costs.
