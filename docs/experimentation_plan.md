# Experimentation Plan

## Objective

Develop a structured testing roadmap to improve funnel conversion performance and increase recurring revenue.

---

# Experiment 1

## Mobile Onboarding Simplification

### Problem

Mobile users convert at a lower rate than desktop users.

### Hypothesis

Reducing onboarding complexity on mobile devices will increase activation and paid conversion rates.

### Proposed Change

Current State:

* Multiple onboarding screens
* Excessive form fields

Future State:

* Simplified onboarding flow
* Reduced data entry requirements

### Success Metrics

Primary Metric:

* Activation Rate

Secondary Metrics:

* Trial Participation Rate
* Paid Conversion Rate

### Expected Outcome

5%–10% increase in activation.

### Priority

High

---

# Experiment 2

## Trial-to-Paid Upgrade Messaging

### Problem

A significant percentage of trial users do not convert to paid subscriptions.

### Hypothesis

Improved upgrade messaging will increase paid conversion rates.

### Proposed Change

Introduce:

* Upgrade prompts
* Benefit-driven messaging
* Feature comparison pages

### Success Metrics

Primary Metric:

* Trial-to-Paid Conversion Rate

Secondary Metrics:

* Monthly Recurring Revenue
* Upgrade Click Rate

### Expected Outcome

3%–7% increase in paid conversions.

### Priority

Highest

---

# Experiment 3

## Channel Landing Page Optimization

### Problem

Conversion quality varies by acquisition channel.

### Hypothesis

Channel-specific landing pages will improve signup and activation rates.

### Proposed Change

Create dedicated landing pages for:

* Organic Search
* Paid Search
* Referral Traffic
* Social Media

### Success Metrics

Primary Metric:

* Signup Rate

Secondary Metrics:

* Activation Rate
* Paid Conversion Rate

### Expected Outcome

5% increase in signup conversion.

### Priority

Medium

### Success Metrics

Primary Metric:

Secondary Metrics:

* Trial Participation Rate

### Expected Outcome

5% increase in activation.

### Priority

Medium

# Experiment 5

* Feature comparisons

Medium
