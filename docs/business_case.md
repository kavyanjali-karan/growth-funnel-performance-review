# Business Case

## Background

A SaaS company has experienced slower-than-expected recurring revenue growth despite maintaining stable website traffic and customer acquisition volumes.

Leadership suspects that inefficiencies within the customer acquisition funnel are preventing users from successfully converting into paying subscribers.

The Business Intelligence team was tasked with identifying where customers drop off, which customer segments underperform, and what revenue opportunities exist through funnel optimization.

---

## Business Problem

While traffic acquisition remains healthy, only a small percentage of users progress through all funnel stages and become paying customers.

Management needs answers to the following questions:

1. Where do customers drop out of the funnel?
2. Which customer segments convert best?
3. Which segments underperform?
4. How much revenue is being lost due to funnel inefficiencies?
5. What initiatives should be prioritized first?

---

## Business Objectives

### Objective 1

Measure end-to-end funnel performance.

Success Metric:

* Visitor-to-Paid Conversion Rate

---

### Objective 2

Identify high-friction funnel stages.

Success Metric:

* Stage-Level Dropoff Rate

---

### Objective 3

Evaluate acquisition channel effectiveness.

Success Metrics:

* Paid Conversion Rate
* Revenue Per User
* Revenue Contribution

---

### Objective 4

Evaluate device performance.

Success Metrics:

* Paid Conversion Rate by Device
* Revenue by Device
* Revenue Opportunity by Device

---

### Objective 5

Estimate recoverable revenue.

Success Metrics:

* Incremental Paid Customers
* Incremental Monthly Revenue
* Revenue Recovery Opportunity

---

## Stakeholders

### VP Revenue

Primary Concern:

Increase recurring revenue growth.

Key Questions:

* How much revenue are we losing?
* Which initiative creates the largest impact?

---

### Product Manager

Primary Concern:

* Which users fail to activate?

Primary Concern:

---

Primary Concern:

Improve acquisition ROI.

Key Questions:

---

## Analytical Approach

The project follows a six-stage analytics workflow:

1. Data Generation
2. Data Cleaning
3. Data Validation
4. Funnel Analysis
5. Revenue Opportunity Analysis
6. Executive Reporting

The analysis evaluates:

* Funnel progression
* Segment performance
* Revenue contribution
* Conversion efficiency
* Opportunity sizing

---

## Expected Business Impact

If underperforming segments are improved to benchmark conversion levels, the organization can:

* Increase paid customer acquisition
* Improve recurring revenue
* Reduce customer acquisition waste
* Improve marketing ROI
* Increase product-led growth efficiency

---

## Executive Summary

The primary growth opportunity is not increasing acquisition volume.

The highest-value opportunity is improving conversion efficiency within the existing funnel.

Small improvements in activation and paid conversion rates can generate meaningful recurring revenue growth without increasing acquisition costs.
