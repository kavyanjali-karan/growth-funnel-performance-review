# Stakeholder Recommendations

## Audience

- VP Revenue
- Product Manager
- Growth Manager
- Marketing Director

---

# Recommendation 1

## Improve Mobile Conversion Experience

Problem:

Mobile users convert below benchmark.

Evidence:

Device-level funnel analysis identified lower paid conversion performance among mobile users.

Business Impact:

Improving mobile conversion rates increases recurring revenue without increasing acquisition spend.

Suggested Actions:

- Reduce onboarding steps
- Simplify registration flow
- Improve page load performance
- Optimize mobile UX

Priority:

High

Effort:

Medium

Expected Impact:

High

# Recommendation 2

## Improve Trial-to-Paid Conversion

Problem:

Largest drop-off occurs after trial enrollment.

Evidence:

Funnel analysis shows significant loss between trial participation and subscription purchase.

Business Impact:

Improves monetization of existing demand.

Suggested Actions:

- Introduce onboarding email sequence
- Add usage milestone reminders
- Improve premium feature visibility
- Test pricing presentation

Priority:

Highest

Effort:

Medium

Expected Impact:

Very High

# Recommendation 3

## Reallocate Marketing Budget

Problem:

Some acquisition channels generate low-value customers.

Evidence:

Channel-level conversion analysis identifies performance variation.

Business Impact:

Increases marketing ROI.

Suggested Actions:

- Increase spend on high-performing channels
- Reduce spend on low-performing channels
- Track revenue-per-user by channel

Priority:

High

Effort:

Low

Expected Impact:

High

# Recommendation 4

## Establish Funnel Monitoring

Problem:

Conversion performance is reviewed reactively.

Evidence:

No ongoing monitoring process currently exists.

Business Impact:

Enables earlier issue detection.

Suggested Actions:

- Create dashboard alerts
- Monitor funnel KPIs weekly
- Track segment-level conversion trends

Priority:

Medium

Effort:

Low

Expected Impact:

Medium

# Recommendation 5

## Implement Cohort Tracking

Problem:

Current reporting focuses on aggregate performance.

Evidence:

Aggregate metrics can hide changing customer quality.

Business Impact:

Provides visibility into long-term performance trends.

Suggested Actions:

- Track monthly cohorts
- Monitor paid conversion by cohort
- Compare acquisition quality over time

Priority:

Medium

Effort:

Medium

Expected Impact:

High
