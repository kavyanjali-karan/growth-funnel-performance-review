# Executive Summary

Analysis of 9,995 customer journeys identified significant inefficiencies within the SaaS acquisition funnel.

The largest opportunity is not increasing traffic volume.

The largest opportunity is improving conversion efficiency among existing users.

A device-level investigation identified a substantial mobile performance gap that is limiting recurring revenue growth.

---

# Business Context

The organization has achieved stable customer acquisition volumes but recurring revenue growth has underperformed expectations.

Leadership requested a funnel analysis to identify:

* Conversion bottlenecks
* Segment performance differences
* Revenue leakage
* Optimization opportunities

---

# Key Findings

## Finding 1

Mobile users convert at significantly lower rates than desktop users.

Business Impact:

Lost recurring revenue due to onboarding friction.

Estimated Opportunity:

$8,828 monthly recurring revenue.

Priority:

Highest

---

## Finding 2

The largest funnel drop occurs between trial and paid conversion.

Business Impact:

Marketing investment generates demand but monetization remains inefficient.

Priority:

Highest

---

## Finding 3

Referral users significantly outperform social-media-acquired users.

Business Impact:

Current marketing spend allocation is suboptimal.

Priority:

High

---

# Strategic Recommendations

### Recommendation 1

Redesign mobile onboarding.

Expected Outcome:

Higher activation and paid conversion.

Impact:

High

Effort:

Medium

---

### Recommendation 2

Improve trial-to-paid conversion.

Expected Outcome:

Increased recurring revenue.

Impact:

High

Effort:

Medium

---

### Recommendation 3

Increase referral acquisition investment.

Expected Outcome:

Higher customer acquisition efficiency.

Impact:

High

Effort:

Low

---

# Expected Business Outcome

If benchmark conversion rates are achieved across underperforming segments:

* Additional paid customers can be acquired without increasing traffic.
* Monthly recurring revenue can increase materially.
* Marketing efficiency improves.
* Customer acquisition costs decrease.

---

# Conclusion

The primary growth opportunity is conversion optimization rather than acquisition expansion.

Investment should prioritize mobile onboarding, trial conversion improvements, and acquisition-channel optimization.
