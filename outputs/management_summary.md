# Management Summary

## Objective

Evaluate the SaaS acquisition funnel and identify opportunities to improve recurring revenue.

---

## Scope

The analysis covered:

* 9,995 users
* Funnel conversion performance
* Device segmentation
* Acquisition channel segmentation
* Revenue opportunity estimation

---

## Key Metrics

| Metric              | Value   |
| ------------------- | ------- |
| Users               | 9,995   |
| Paid Customers      | 277     |
| Funnel Conversion   | 2.8%    |
| Monthly Revenue     | $19,563 |
| Revenue Opportunity | $8,828  |

---

## Major Findings

### Mobile Performance Gap

Mobile users significantly underperform desktop users.

Recommendation:

Improve mobile onboarding experience.

---

### Trial Conversion Bottleneck

A substantial number of trial users fail to become paying customers.

Recommendation:

Improve upgrade experience and trial engagement.

---

### Channel Quality Gap

Referral traffic significantly outperforms social media traffic.

Recommendation:

Reallocate marketing budget toward referral acquisition.

---

## Recommended Priorities

### Priority 1

Mobile onboarding redesign.

Expected Impact:

Very High

---

### Priority 2

Trial-to-paid conversion optimization.

Expected Impact:

Very High

---

### Priority 3

Marketing budget reallocation.

Expected Impact:

High

---

## Executive Recommendation

Focus investment on funnel optimization before increasing acquisition spend.

The analysis indicates that improving conversion efficiency offers the highest return on investment and the fastest path to recurring revenue growth.
